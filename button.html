    Sampling

    Our cooperated garment factory has over 15 workers dedicated to making fitting samples and pre-production samples. We want samples to be done as quickly as you. The more quick we get our samples done, the more nice products we can make each season. The sample room team can usually process samples in familiar fabrics within one week. If the sampling fabric is a bit hard to get, it may take a bit more time. Our merchandisers will always update you on when your samples are expected to be ready.

    patterning and sampling

    Our sample room staff is fully qualified to do patterning. If you prefer to submit your own patterns, this is of course no problem at all. The sample room has software to read most digital files.

    Sampling and bulk production are a package deal. Sampling will start after we have an agreement with the buyer on all conditions such as price and delivery date for the bulk production. Once we are confident we are starting a nice project together we will make as many samples as it takes to get the product right.

    Sustainability

    The fashion industry unfortunately leaves a big carbon footprint. It will take quite some time & energy before the industry has made the transition to green. The solutions are in reducing waste (or zero waste) and innovation. Savvy Garments have the ultimate goal to be a “zero waste” clothing manufacturer. What’s more, we are in the constant pursuit of innovation in materials. 

    We are lucky to live in an era of constant research and innovation. From Austria to China, new materials are being developed and traditional materials are being improved. Research and technology is shared between buyers and suppliers. Working together, brands and garment manufacturers work on a more green fashion industry. 

    Shared Resources

    At Savvy Garments, we think it’s important to share all our resources for sustainability with our clients. Besides that, we also actively source any new sustainable material requested by our clients. If apparel manufacturers and apparel buyers work together, the industry can make quick progress when it comes to sustainable clothing production. 

    At the moment we have running developments in sustainable materials such as wool, linen, lyocell, organic cotton, Eco Vero, Livaeco, and Circulose. We have the resources to supply our clients with sustainable materials as long as they are available in China.

    FAQ

    Do you have an MOQ?

    We do work with MOQ’s, because we do mass production and buy newly made materials directly from the mill. It’s not possible to offer good supply of materials and stable production quality without meeting the minimums. Minimums depend on the fabric, but on average we will need around 500 pieces per style per colour to start with.

    Where are you guys located?

    We are located in Hangzhou, Zhejiang. That’s the eastern part of China and about one hour away from Shanghai by train.

    How do we get started?

    You can reach out to use via our contact from. Please tell us a bit more about your brand, your selling format, your products and so on. If it looks like we are the right clothing manufacturer for your brand we can start out with some costing exercise and trial orders.

    What certifications do you have?

    Our clothing factories participate in the BSCI monitoring system. If you are an Amfori member you can see all yearly audit reports through the Amfori platform.

    To which countries do you ship?

    We work under FOB terms and do not offer DDP or FCL terms. Our export markets are mainly Europe and North America.

    What are your payment terms?

    The terms are 30% deposit and 70% T/T against docs.
